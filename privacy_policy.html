Privacy Policy for SQL Command App

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

SQL Command App is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our app:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Display Name: Optional name for your profile
  • Profile Photo: Optional photo URL (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Authentication Data: Secure authentication tokens managed by Firebase

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app:

  • SQL Queries: All SQL and PL/SQL queries you execute are stored locally and synced to the cloud
  • Query History: Timestamps, execution results, and error logs of your queries
  • Points and Achievements: Your earned points, completed tasks, and daily rewards
  • Table Schemas: Metadata of tables you create within the app
  • App Activity: Features you use and interaction patterns

Device Information

We may collect:

  • Device Type: Mobile device model and operating system version
  • App Version: Version of SQL Command App you're using
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and technical diagnostics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  1. Account Management: Creating and managing your user account
  2. Feature Functionality: Enabling core features like query execution, cloud sync, and leaderboard
  3. Personalization: Customizing your experience based on your preferences
  4. AI Assistance: Processing your queries with Google Gemini AI for suggestions and explanations
  5. Progress Tracking: Maintaining your points, achievements, and activity history
  6. App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs
  7. Security: Protecting against unauthorized access and fraudulent activities

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: User authentication (email/password and Google Sign-In)
  • Data Shared: Email, display name, authentication tokens
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Supabase

  • Purpose: Cloud database storage and synchronization
  • Data Shared: User profile, query history, table schemas, points data
  • Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: AI-powered SQL assistance and query explanation
  • Data Shared: SQL queries you request help with (sent to Gemini API)
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: Optional authentication method
  • Data Shared: Email, name, profile photo
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Query history, tasks, and user-created tables are stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • Local data is encrypted using device-level security

Cloud Storage

  • User data is securely stored in Supabase with row-level security (RLS)
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Firebase handles authentication securely with industry-standard protocols

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Row-level security policies in Supabase
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Query History: You can clear your query history at any time from within the app
  • Local Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access: View all data we have about you
  2. Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  3. Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  4. Export: Download your query history and data in CSV format
  5. Opt-Out: Stop using cloud sync features (data remains local only)

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Update Profile: Use the Profile screen in the app
  • Clear History: Use "Clear History" option in the app
  • Export Data: Use the Import/Export feature in the app

Children's Privacy

SQL Command App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data for advertising purposes
  • Use your SQL queries for any purpose other than providing you service

We MAY share your information only in these cases:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties (Firebase, Supabase, Google AI) as described above
  • Leaderboard: Your display name and points are publicly visible on the leaderboard

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the app, you consent to such transfers. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place.

Cookies and Tracking

SQL Command App does not use cookies. We use:

  • Local Storage: For app functionality on your device
  • Authentication Tokens: To keep you signed in
  • Analytics: Basic usage analytics to improve the app (no personal identification)

Consent

By using SQL Command App, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Summary of Key Points

✅ What we collect: Email, display name, query history, usage data
✅ Why we collect: To provide app features, sync data, and improve your experience
✅ Third parties: Firebase (auth), Supabase (storage), Google Gemini (AI)
✅ Your control: You can delete data, export history, or delete your account anytime
✅ Security: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
✅ No selling: We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store Requirements
  • Firebase and Supabase Privacy Standards
