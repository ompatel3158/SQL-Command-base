Account & Data Deletion Policy

Account Deletion Overview

SQL Command App developed by [Your Name/Company Name] respects your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your data or your entire account at any time using the methods below.

How to Delete Your Account

You have three options to request account deletion:

✅ Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open SQL Command App
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap the menu icon (⋮) in the top-right corner
  4. Select "Delete Account or Data"
  5. Choose one of:
    • Delete Data (Keep Account) - Removes your query history and tables but keeps your account active
    • Delete Account (All Data) - Permanently removes everything including your account
  6. Confirm your action when prompted

📧 Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to support@example.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request - SQL Command App"
  • Include: The email address associated with your account
  • Specify: Whether you want to delete only data or your entire account

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email.

🌐 Option 3: Web Form (Coming Soon)

A web-based deletion request form will be available at this URL in the near future for users who prefer not to use the app or email.

What Data is Deleted

✔️ Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Query History: All your saved SQL queries and execution history
  • User Tables: Any custom tables you created in the app
  • Leaderboard Presence: Your profile will be removed from public leaderboards
  • Account Credentials: Your email and authentication data (if full account deletion)
  • Profile Settings: All preferences and configurations

📌 Data Retention Period

Deleted data may be retained in backup systems for 30-90 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes. After this period, all data is permanently removed from our systems.

⚠️ Important: Deletion is Permanent

Once your data is deleted and the retention period expires, it cannot be recovered. Make sure to export any important queries or tables before proceeding.

Partial Data Deletion

You can delete your query history and custom tables without deleting your account. This allows you to keep using the app with a fresh start while maintaining your login credentials.

To do this, select "Delete Data (Keep Account)" option in the app's Profile menu.

Verification

For security purposes, we may require identity verification for deletion requests made via email or web form. In-app deletion requests are automatically verified through your active login session.

Questions or Issues?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or encounter any issues, please contact us at support@example.com
